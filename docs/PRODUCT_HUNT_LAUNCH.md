# Product Hunt Launch Guide

This guide helps you prepare for a successful Product Hunt launch of DataStory AI.

## Pre-Launch Preparation (2-4 Weeks Before)

### 1. Product Hunt Profile Setup

- [ ] Create/update Product Hunt account
- [ ] Add professional profile photo
- [ ] Write compelling bio
- [ ] Link social media accounts
- [ ] Build reputation by commenting and upvoting

### 2. Hunter Outreach

- [ ] Identify potential hunters with relevant audience
- [ ] Reach out 2-3 weeks before launch
- [ ] Provide early access for testing
- [ ] Share product vision and story

### 3. Assets Preparation

#### Product Images
- [ ] Hero image (1270x760px)
- [ ] Gallery images (4-6 images, 1270x760px each)
- [ ] Screenshots of key features
- [ ] Demo GIF or video

#### Copy
- [ ] Tagline (60 characters max)
- [ ] Description (260 characters)
- [ ] First comment (detailed explanation)
- [ ] Maker comment

### 4. Community Building

- [ ] Build email list of supporters
- [ ] Engage with Product Hunt community
- [ ] Join relevant Slack/Discord communities
- [ ] Prepare social media posts

## Launch Day Strategy

### Timing

**Best Launch Time:**
- Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday
- 12:01 AM PST (Product Hunt day starts)
- Avoid Mondays, Fridays, weekends
- Avoid major holidays

### Hour-by-Hour Plan

**12:00 AM - 1:00 AM PST**
- [ ] Submit product to Product Hunt
- [ ] Post first comment as maker
- [ ] Share on Twitter
- [ ] Email supporters list
- [ ] Post in relevant Slack/Discord communities

**1:00 AM - 8:00 AM PST**
- [ ] Monitor comments and respond
- [ ] Thank supporters
- [ ] Share updates on social media
- [ ] Engage with other launches

**8:00 AM - 12:00 PM PST** (Peak Hours)
- [ ] Actively respond to all comments
- [ ] Share on LinkedIn
- [ ] Post in relevant subreddits (if allowed)
- [ ] Engage with Product Hunt community
- [ ] Monitor upvotes and ranking

**12:00 PM - 5:00 PM PST**
- [ ] Continue responding to comments
- [ ] Share user testimonials
- [ ] Post updates on progress
- [ ] Thank top supporters publicly

**5:00 PM - 11:59 PM PST**
- [ ] Final push on social media
- [ ] Respond to remaining comments
- [ ] Thank everyone for support
- [ ] Prepare next-day follow-up

### Building Momentum

**Early Supporters (First 50 Upvotes):**
- Personal thank you messages
- Offer extended trial or special features
- Ask for detailed feedback

**Mid-Day Push (Hours 8-12):**
- Share milestone updates ("100 upvotes! üéâ")
- Post user testimonials
- Share interesting use cases

**Final Push (Hours 18-24):**
- Thank everyone publicly
- Share final ranking
- Tease upcoming features

## Post-Launch Follow-Up

### Day 2-7

- [ ] Thank you email to supporters
- [ ] Share launch results on social media
- [ ] Write blog post about launch experience
- [ ] Respond to remaining comments
- [ ] Follow up with interested users
- [ ] Implement quick wins from feedback
- [ ] Plan next steps based on feedback

### Metrics to Track

- Total upvotes
- Final ranking (#1-10 is excellent)
- Comments and engagement
- Website traffic spike
- User signups
- Story generations
- Conversion rate
- Social media engagement
- Press mentions
- Backlinks generated

### Success Criteria

**Great Launch:**
- Top 5 product of the day
- 300+ upvotes
- 50+ comments
- 1000+ website visits
- 100+ signups

**Good Launch:**
- Top 10 product of the day
- 200+ upvotes
- 30+ comments
- 500+ website visits
- 50+ signups
