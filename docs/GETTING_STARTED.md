# Getting Started with DataStory AI

This quick start guide will help you create your first data story in under 5 minutes.

## Prerequisites

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- A CSV or Excel file with your data
- An email address for account creation

## Quick Start (5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Create Your Account (1 minute)

1. Go to [datastory.ai](https://datastory.ai)
2. Click **"Sign Up"**
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Click **"Create Account"**

‚úÖ You're now logged in and on your dashboard!

### Step 2: Prepare Your Data (2 minutes)

Your data should be in CSV or Excel format with:
- **Column headers** in the first row
- **At least 10 rows** of data
- **At least 2 columns**
- **Under 1,000 rows** (free tier)

**Don't have data?** Download one of our sample datasets:
- [Sales Data](../public/sample-datasets/sales-data.csv)
- [Customer Analytics](../public/sample-datasets/customer-analytics.csv)
- [Marketing Campaign](../public/sample-datasets/marketing-campaign.csv)

### Step 3: Upload and Generate (1 minute)

1. Click **"Create New Story"** on your dashboard
2. **Drag and drop** your file or click **"Browse"**
3. Click **"Upload and Generate Story"**
4. Wait 30-60 seconds while we analyze your data

### Step 4: Explore Your Story (1 minute)

## Example: Sales Analysis

Let's walk through a real example using sales data.

### Your Data

```csv
Date,Sales,Region
2024-01-01,15000,North
2024-01-02,18000,South
2024-01-03,12000,East
...
```

### What You'll Get

**Summary:**
> "This dataset contains 31 days of sales data across 4 regions. Total sales reached $589,000 with an average daily revenue of $19,000..."

**Key Findings:**
> "Sales show a strong upward trend with 15% growth over the period. The West region consistently outperforms others by 25%..."

**Recommendations:**
> "Focus marketing efforts on the West region's successful strategies. Investigate the East region's lower performance..."

**Visualizations:**
- Line chart showing sales trend over time
- Bar chart comparing regions
- Pie chart showing regional distribution

## Tips for Better Results

### ‚úÖ Do This

- Use descriptive column names ("Monthly_Revenue" not "Col1")
- Include units in names ("Revenue_USD", "Weight_KG")
- Clean your data before uploading
- Use standard date formats (YYYY-MM-DD)

### ‚ùå Avoid This

- Special characters in column names
- Missing or duplicate headers
- Inconsistent data formats
- Unnecessary columns

## Troubleshooting

### "File too large" error
- Reduce your dataset to 1,000 rows or less
- Remove unnecessary columns
- Consider upgrading for larger datasets

### "Invalid format" error
- Ensure file is .csv, .xlsx, or .xls
- Check that file isn't corrupted
- Re-save from Excel if needed

### Processing takes too long
- Wait up to 2 minutes
- Refresh if stuck
- Try uploading again

### Charts don't appear
- Enable JavaScript
- Try a different browser
- Clear browser cache
